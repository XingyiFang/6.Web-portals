NSF EarthCube Integration and Testing Environment
Overview

Services: Integration and collaboration

The earthCube integration and test environment enables users to integrate their software and hardware resources and manage the integration process. Users can control the deployment and management of the resources without managing hardware or middleware directly by the integration tool.

Users can deploy their applications and services on the cloud and integrate them with existing cloud-based applications. It can quickly deploy cloud-based applications to adapt to the needs of integration by using portals for monitoring and controlling integration procedures. In other words, it is the earthCube integration and test environment, not the user that controls the execution of integrations. The details of integrations are informed to users and they can control the whole process.

The earthCube integration and test environment helps to improve the efficiency of the project team in which the team members can be more productive. The team members can access internal and external resources anywhere form any device. The platform provides the rights management services which helps the team manager to manage the project and the members of the team more productive. The team members can upload, co-edit, comment, share and download the documents form the cloud platform according to their corresponding rights anytime. With the help of the earthCube integration and test environment, the term members can collaborate online without the need of face to face meeting at any time. People can cooperate with different people all over the word by any RISC or CISC based devices.
